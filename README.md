# 🎓 Student Academic Performance Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview
In the educational sector, understanding the factors that influence student success is essential for providing timely support. This project leverages **Machine Learning (Linear Regression)** to predict a student's **Performance Index** based on their study habits, previous academic history, and lifestyle factors.

## 💼 Business Value
By accurately predicting student outcomes, educational institutions can:
* **Identify At-Risk Students:** Provide early intervention for students predicted to have low performance.
* **Resource Allocation:** Understand which factors (e.g., study hours vs. sleep) have the most impact on success.
* **Personalized Guidance:** Offer data-driven advice to students to help improve their academic results.
